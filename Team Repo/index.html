Gallery

This is a Gallery component. It can behave as a lightbox (when given the lightbox class), and you can customize its appearance with a number of modifiers, as well as assign it an optional onload or onscroll transition modifier (details).

Items

This is an Items component, and it's essentially a grid for organising elements of various types. You can customize its appearance with a choice of modifiers, as well as assign it an optional onload or onscroll transition modifier (details).
